<p class="lede">Signposts runs on Signposts. This is the live listing of <strong>this repo's own</strong> bundles, signs and rules — generated at build time from its <code>signposts.yml</code>, so it can never drift from what actually ships.</p>

Each entry's one-line summary is its `description:` field. A <Chip kind="sign">sign</Chip> steers (it never blocks); a <Chip kind="rule">rule</Chip> blocks at the [moment](/docs/wiring) shown. The bundles below are the four the project ships: **core** (what a fresh install carries), **local** (dogfood guards for working on Signposts), and the installable **git-hygiene** and **justfile** bundles.
